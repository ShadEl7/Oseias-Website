Our Programs

Robin's Children's Choir offers a variety of programs to nurture young voices and foster a love for music in children of all ages.

Our Core Programs

Explore our range of programs designed to inspire and develop the musical talents of children.

Beginner Choir

Perfect for young children just starting their musical journey. Learn the basics of singing and rhythm in a fun, supportive environment.

  • Introduction to Music
  • Basic Vocal Techniques
  • Fun Group Activities

Advanced Choir

For experienced young singers looking to refine their skills. Focus on advanced vocal techniques and performance preparation.

  • Advanced Vocal Training
  • Performance Opportunities
  • Music Theory

Summer Camp

A week-long immersive experience filled with music, fun, and friendship. Perfect for children of all skill levels.

  • Workshops & Activities
  • Group Performances
  • Memorable Experiences

Private Lessons

One-on-one coaching tailored to your child's unique needs. Focus on individual growth and skill development.

  • Personalized Training
  • Flexible Scheduling
  • Goal-Oriented Approach

Auditions

Showcase your singing skills and join Robin's Children's Choir! Sign up for an audition today.

We welcome children of all skill levels to audition for our choir. Auditions are a great way to assess your child's vocal abilities and find the best program for them.

  • Auditions are held every Saturday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM.
  • Location: Robin's Children's Choir Studio, 123 Music Lane, Harmony City.
  • Prepare a short song to perform during the audition.
